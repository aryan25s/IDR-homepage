# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) — Brand & Homepage

This project designs a **brand identity and responsive homepage** for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)**, an industry-led institute focused on **digital, cyber and AI risk training and deployment**.

---

## Logo Design

The IDR logo uses a **cube-inspired geometric icon** to represent **structure, resilience and layered digital risk management**.  
The cube reflects how digital risk frameworks are built in **multiple structured layers**, similar to cybersecurity and AI governance systems.

The colour palette follows the assignment requirement:

- **Orange (#FF6A00)** – Innovation, technology and forward thinking in AI and cyber risk  
- **Black (#000000)** – Security, strength and trust  
- **White (#FFFFFF)** – Clarity and simplicity  

The logo is designed in a **minimalist style** so it remains **clear and legible at small sizes**, making it suitable for use as a **favicon, mobile header icon or website logo**.

Two logo variants were created:

1. **Icon Only Version**
   - Used for favicon, mobile header or small UI elements.

2. **Icon + Text Version**
   - Displays the full name **“Institute of Digital Risk”** alongside the cube icon.
   - Suitable for website headers, presentations and documents.

---

## Homepage

The responsive homepage includes:

- **Hero Section** – mission statement and call-to-action  
- **About IDR** – explains the institute and its academic-industry collaboration  
- **Service Pillars** – Academy, Innovation & Incubation, Advisory Services  
- **Community Section** – students, professionals and organisations  
- **Contact Form** – register interest or partnership enquiries
