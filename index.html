About IDR

Bridging Academia and Industry in Digital Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training institute focused on cyber, technology and AI risk roles in regulated environments.

IDR connects academic research with real-world industry practice through partnerships with universities and organisations.

Our Model

Three Pillars of Digital Risk Excellence

🎯

Academy

Bootcamps and training programs for digital risk, cyber security and AI governance.

💡

Innovation & Incubation

Developing risk models, AI governance frameworks and research collaboration.

🛡️

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Who We Serve

IDR supports students, early-career professionals and organisations in sectors like financial services, government and infrastructure.
